🚀 Deploy Your Recruitment Scoreboard

Choose the easiest option for you (all are FREE)

Option 1: Netlify Drop Easiest

Drag and drop your file to get it online in 30 seconds. No account needed!

📤 Open Netlify Drop

📋 What to do after clicking:

  1. A new tab will open with a box
  2. Find "DEMO.html" file in your Downloads\Scoreboard folder
  3. Drag the file and drop it in the box
  4. Wait 5 seconds - you'll get your link!
  5. Copy and share the link
OR

Option 2: Tiiny Host No Drag

Just click buttons - no dragging needed. Super simple!

🌐 Open Tiiny Host

📋 What to do after clicking:

  1. Click "Choose File" button on the website
  2. Select "DEMO.html" from Downloads\Scoreboard folder
  3. Click "Upload" button
  4. Wait a few seconds - you'll get your link!
  5. Copy and share the link
OR

Option 3: Surge.sh Alternative

Another simple option with instant deployment.

⚡ Learn About Surge

📋 What to do:

  1. Follow the simple guide on their website
  2. Upload DEMO.html
  3. Get your link

💡 Important Information

✅ All your data (positions, candidates, resumes) is saved inside DEMO.html
✅ No account required for Options 1 & 2
✅ Completely FREE forever
✅ Your scoreboard will work exactly the same online
✅ You can use the link on any device (phone, tablet, laptop)
